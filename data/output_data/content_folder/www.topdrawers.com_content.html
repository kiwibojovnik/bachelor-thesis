 Welcome to Topdrawers - Gear for Men!

Topdrawers is located in downtown Vancouver, BC Canada, as a storefront since 1992 and serving online customers since 1998!  We pride ourselves on being one of the first mens "underwear stores", both in a bricks and mortar setting as well as online.  We attribute our longevity to our amazing, loyal customer base, and to always seeking new products and new ways to make shopping easier.   We have been voted one of the best men's clothing/menswear retailers in downtown Vancouver twice by the readers of the West Ender newspaper. 

Men's Underwear, Swimwear & Activewear apparel! Topdrawers features a wide selection of good underwear brands for men by Andrew Christian, PUMP!, Addicted, ES Collection, Gregg Homme, Garçon, Joe Snyder, Private Structure, JM, Modus Vivendi, TOF Paris, Saxx, CellBlock13, Cocksox, Nasty Pig, GYM Jockstraps, Obviously, Clever Moda, Go Softwear, and more, with new arrivals daily.

In addition we also offer men's swimwear, casual clothing, workout wear, fetish wear, and accessories.  Thank you for visiting - have fun shopping!
