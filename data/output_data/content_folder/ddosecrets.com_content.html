      Distributed Denial of Secrets

      From Distributed Denial of Secrets

      Distributed Denial of Secrets (DDoSecrets) is a 501(c)(3) journalist non-profit that publishes and archives leaks in the public interest. Thanks to our sources and donors, DDoSecrets has published over 100 million files in 5 years.

      We ensure that information is available to those who need it most—the people. You can read more about us and our publications on Wikipedia, but our mission is simple:

      Know the truth, no matter what - Veritatem cognoscere ruat cælum et pereat mundus.
