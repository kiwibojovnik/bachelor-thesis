FAQs

What is included in a subscription?

For just /month, a subscription provides access to millions of ebooks, audiobooks, magazines, documents, professional content, and more across three unique products:

  • Everand offers millions of ebooks, audiobooks, magazines, newspapers, podcasts and sheet music.
  • Scribd hosts millions of community-uploaded documents, such as academic papers, legal documents, manuals, and more.
  • SlideShare showcases millions of community-uploaded presentations and professional documents.

What is Scribd?

Scribd is a digital document library, with over 170M documents and counting. Find and upload text on every topic and niche, from academic papers and legal documents, to DIY hobbies, manuals, and more. To share a document to Scribd, you must be the creator to or have the permission of the documents creator. Read more on that in our Copyright FAQs.

What is Everand?

Everand is your new home for stories and knowledge. For just /month, you can explore millions of ebooks, audiobooks, podcasts, magazines, newspapers, sheet music, and so much more. Read what you want, how you want across devices and even offline.

Can I still find ebooks, audiobooks, podcasts, and magazines on Scribd?

Titles like ebooks, audiobooks, podcasts, and magazines are now available on Everand. You can find documents like academic papers, legal documents, manuals, and more on Scribd.

How do I access Everand?

Visit Everand.com or download the Everand app (available for iOS and Android) to gain access to millions of titles. If youre already a Scribd subscriber, Everand is included in your monthly subscription. Log into Everand with your Scribd username and password.
