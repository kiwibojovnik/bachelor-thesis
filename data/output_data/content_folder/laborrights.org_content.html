2024 Global Labor Rights Defenders Celebration

Our Global Labor Rights Defenders Celebration is an annual gathering of friends, partners, and supporters in the labor movement to celebrate worker organizing around the world and support labor rights defenders putting themselves at risk every day to advance justice and democracy in their communities. This year, we’re very excited to be able to host this event on May Day and join our collective voice to workers around the city and around the world in demanding a better future for all. 

Global Labor Justice - International Labor Rights Forum’s 2024 Global Labor Rights Defenders Celebration on Wednesday, May 1.
