About Us

Hall-Proctor Institute

Hall-Proctor Institute 2023 brought together advocates, thinkers, and leaders to explore pathways for racial and economic justice. This transformative gathering at CDF Haley Farm delves into crucial discussions, fostering collaboration and inspiring action for a more equitable future.

Learn More

Young People Lead Podcast

The podcast aims to demonstrate how young people can and should lead by telling their stories of how youth are transforming policies for the better and examining research on the policies that most affect them.

Learn More

CDF Celebrates 50 Years

Children's Defense Fund's 2023 Policy Forum and Celebration of Joy at Howard University, Washington, D.C., marked our 50th and 30th Anniversaries of CDF Freedom Schools®. This event, focusing on health, early childhood development, communities, economic mobility, and civic engagement, united various community members for two days of learning, networking, and growth.

Learn More

2024 NBA HBCU Fellowship Program

The NBA Foundation selects Children's Defense Fund to manage the NBA HBCU Fellowship Program in a three-year partnership launched in January 2023.

Learn More

30 Years of CDF Freedom Schools®

In 2023, CDF Freedom Schools® National Training united passionate educators, advocates, and youth leaders. This annual event equips attendees with tools, knowledge, and a shared commitment to children and youth, promoting literacy and creating positive change in communities nationwide.
