Putting opportunity within reach

For more than 50 years, FHI 360 has been advancing equity, health and well-being through data-driven, locally led solutions to improve lives today and build a more equitable tomorrow.

Our strategy

Who we are

FHI 360 is a global organization that mobilizes research, resources and relationships so that people everywhere can access the opportunities they need to lead full, healthy lives.

  • Areas of impact

    Advancing equity and social cohesion

    Declines in social cohesion can magnify inequality and threaten democracy, peace and stability worldwide. Drawing on more than 50 years of research, resources and relationships, FHI 360 helps build more cohesive communities, where opportunities are within reach for all.
  • Areas of impact

    Addressing growing global health threats

    Growing health crises make it more urgent than ever to discover breakthroughs, heal people and improve lives. By advancing research and evidence-based solutions, FHI 360 helps ensure a healthier future for people everywhere.
  • Areas of impact

    Preventing and responding to crises

    Violent conflict and climate crises threaten people’s safety, dignity, health and well-being. Tapping into decades of partnerships and expertise, FHI 360 helps communities affected by crisis meet urgent needs today and build a more resilient tomorrow.
