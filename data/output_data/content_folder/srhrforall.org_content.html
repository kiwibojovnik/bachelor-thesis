Mission

We are dedicated to expanding access to rights-based sexual and reproductive health care, including contraceptive services, STI detection and treatment, cancer screening and treatment, care for survivors of gender-based violence, and safe abortion services; reaching young people with comprehensive sexuality education; and advocating for sexual and reproductive justice. Our approach of building feminist solidarity among organizations across the Global South is central to achieving this mission.
