Kaleidoscope Trust

We work for a world where LGBTI+ people are free, safe and equal everywhere.

Donate
What we do Our impact Our policy manifesto Support us

In 65 countries around the world, LGBTI+ people are criminalised because of who they are and who they love. In half of those countries, homophobic laws originally passed during Britain’s colonial rule have yet to be repealed.

Kaleidoscope Trust is a UK-based international charity fighting for a future where LGBTI+ people everywhere can live free, safe and equal lives.
