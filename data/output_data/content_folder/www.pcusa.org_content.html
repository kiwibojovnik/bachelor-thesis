Pentecost Offering

A FOUNDATION OF FAITH established during childhood through young adulthood helps ensure lifelong faith and service. When you give to the Pentecost Offering, you are helping to provide opportunities for young people to grow and share in their faith in Christ.

New books celebrate supportive communities!

Saving Delicia celebrates the connections between generations, emphasizing that small steps can have a big impact when one looks beyond the present. The Dress in the Window brims with the infectious joy that comes when a child’s individuality is met with wholehearted love. Both books have additional free resources for families and churches.
