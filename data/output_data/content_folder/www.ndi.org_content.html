BY THE NUMBERS

35

Years of experience

NDI has supported democratic institutions and practices in every region of the world for more than three decades.

28,525

Local partners

NDI has supported the efforts of more than 16,000 civic organizations, 300 citizen election monitoring organizations, 925 political parties and organizations, 10,000 legislators and 1,300 women’s organizations.

156

Countries and territories around the world

NDI's work has spanned 156 countries and territories around the world.

600k

Party poll-watchers

NDI has trained 600,000 party poll-watchers in more than 50 countries.

400

candidate debates

NDI has helped partner groups organize more than 400 candidate debates in more than 35 countries.
