50 years making change happen

We have over 50 years’ experience running practical education projects that support people, especially women, who often missed out on school or training opportunities due to extreme poverty, conflict and/or discrimination.

Our Work

Our Thematic Areas

Our adult education projects focus on three themes, all including an element of literacy skills training.

Citizenship

Improving women and people’s knowledge of and access to their political, civic, and human rights.

Economic Empowerment

Improving the financial security and resilience of women and people who have been marginalised.

Health Education

Improving women and people’s knowledge of and access to safe healthcare.
