We are the largest humanitarian organization saving and changing lives worldwide

We bring life-saving relief in emergencies and use food assistance to build a pathway to peace, stability and prosperity for people recovering from conflict, disasters and the impacts of climate change.

Who we are

Learn about our mission, activities, leadership, history and partners.

Read more

WFP in numbers

  • 160 Million people received assistance from WFP in 2022
  • 6.5K trucks 140 aircraft and 20 ships are on the go on any given day
  • 20 million children received nutritious meals, school snacks or take-home rations in 2022
