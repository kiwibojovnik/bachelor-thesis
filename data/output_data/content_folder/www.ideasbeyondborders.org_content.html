Good Ideas Where They Are Needed Most.

"The greatest threat to extremism isn't drones firing missiles, but girls reading books."

-Nicholas D. Kristof

Founded by two immigrants to the United States from Iraq and Singapore who made their life mission to make critical thinking, liberty and science accessible to people worldwide.

Ideas Beyond Borders is a global network of more than 250 translators and changemakers who empower people with good ideas to generate positive impact in their societies.

Our progress so far

Our curated content has been viewed over 215 million times, and our 200+ strong team of translators have helped double the size of Wikipedia Arabic.

Ideas Beyond Borders is a nonprofit with Special Consultative Status to the United Nations. We are committed to upholding critical thinking, science and civil rights in the greater Middle East region.

We are a platinum-level Candid (formerly Guidestar) participant, demonstrating our commitment to transparency with our programs and donors.

Online donations are processed securely through Stripe (credit cards, debit cards & ACH), or Paypal.
