The Intersections Series

Our Intersections series takes a deeper look at the most recent European Fundamental Rights Agency (FRA) survey of LGBTI people, zooming in on the experiences of specific groups within the LGBTI community.

We have published nine reports, on lesbians, youth, older LGBTI people, intersex, trans and non-binary people, racialised LGBTI people, bisexual people, homelessness, and LGBTI people with disabilities.
