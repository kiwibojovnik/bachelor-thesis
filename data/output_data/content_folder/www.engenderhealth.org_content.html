Our Impact

In collaboration with our partners, during our 2022-2023 program year EngenderHealth programs have:

Served an estimated 1,164,500 people with contraceptive care

Prevented about 2,300 maternal deaths

Averted an estimated 477,400 unsafe abortions

Prevented about 31,300 child deaths

Averted an estimated 1,682,900 unintended pregnancies

Saved about $157,679,500
in direct healthcare costs
