Posteo: Maximum Privacy

We do not collect any personal data to protect you from theft and to support your right of informational self-determination.
  • No collection of personal data
  • No connection of payment data to an account
  • No tracking, no user profiling, no trading of customer data
  • Stored data can be encrypted at the touch of a button
  • Security backups, highly efficient virus filter
  • No collection of personal IP addresses
  • Servers located in highly secured German data centres

Posteo: Innovative Encryption

We have a comprehensive encryption and security concept. All data can be encrypted at the touch of a button - even for laypeople.
  • Secure transport route encryption (TLS, DANE and more)
  • TLS-receiving and TLS-sending guarantee (mandatory TLS)
  • Inbound encryption with PGP or S/MIME
  • Crypto mail storage for the entire mailbox
  • Two-factor login with additional email account protection
  • Encryption of calendars, address book and notes
  • PGP in webmail interface, key directory
  • Encrypted hard drives

  • No analysis of user behaviour
  • No integration of social media plug-ins or Google products
