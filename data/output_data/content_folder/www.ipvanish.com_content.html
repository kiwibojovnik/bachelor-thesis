Frequently asked questions

Need help with something? Here are our most frequently asked questions.

1. What is IPVanish?

IPVanish is a virtual private network service, also known as a VPN. A VPN tunnels your internet traffic, masks your true IP address, and shields your data from third parties, cybercriminals, and snoopers.

Without a VPN to encrypt your network traffic, sensitive personal information, and digital rights are constantly put at risk. With IPVanish, youll have online privacy, security, and freedom on any network, from the coffee shop to your home setup, and everywhere in between.

2. What is a VPN?

Short for virtual private network, a VPN is a technology used to secure connections from a device to the internet. It secures connections through the process of encryption to make traffic data illegible to potential onlookers.

3. What is IPVanish used for?

IPVanish provides a secure environment for everyday internet activity. Once you establish a VPN connection, all of your online traffic (web browsing, video streaming, messaging, file-sharing, etc.) passes through our encrypted tunnel, while your identifying IP address is concealed.

4. How much is IPVanish?

Individual IPVanish VPN plans retail at $12.99 for a Monthly plan, $89.99 for a Yearly plan (avg. $7.49/mo), and $179.99 for a 2-Year plan (avg. $7.49/mo).

From time to time, IPVanish VPN offers promotional rates listed on the pricing page and discounted offers on the Coupons page. College students and educators may also take advantage of the Student Discount.

5. How many devices can I protect with IPVanish?

IPVanish VPN does not meter VPN connections, so you can protect every device you own at the same time all from one account.

6. Is IPVanish free?

Free VPNs come with all kinds of drawbacks, including the risk of data theft, slow speeds, annoying ads, and limited features. With IPVanish you wont have to worry about any of that.
