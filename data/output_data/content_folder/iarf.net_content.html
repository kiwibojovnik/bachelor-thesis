Membership
Our membership spans across Africa, East and South Asia, Europe & The Middle East, North America, and Oceania. It includes Buddhists, Christians, Jews, Muslims, Pagans, Shintoists, Unitarians, Zoroastrians, and others. Since 1900, we have been dedicated to providing a platform for interfaith dialogue, a collaborative framework for our members and advocating for those whose religious freedom is infringed upon by states and those whose freedoms are constrained by religions.
