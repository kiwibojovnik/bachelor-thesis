Call for Proposals

24th Call for Proposal

24th Call for Proposals is now closed. However, 25th Call for Proposals is coming soon.

If you think you are in a position to make a difference in the lives and livings of smallholders, consider applying for our fund.

We are looking for agripreneurs/ SMEs (Small and Medium Enterprises)/ Cooperatives/ government offices for alleviation of poverty to apply for CFC finance to make a difference.

Keep an eye for our forthcoming 25th Call for Proposals which will be announced soon. Apply fast and ACT fast to make a difference.

Read more

Mission

To contribute to poverty alleviation by strengthening the income-generating capacity of commodity producers and mitigating vulnerability to their economic well being.

Vision

To strengthen and diversify the commodity sector in developing countries and transform it to be a major contributor to poverty alleviation and sustained economic growth and development.
