SUDAN: CHILDREN’S EMERGENCY APPEAL

The ongoing fighting in Sudan is already having an untold impact on children’s lives. Our teams across the region are urgently preparing to respond to keep children and girls safe and protected. Your support can help us meet the immediate needs of families affected by the crisis.

We know from previous emergencies that children, and especially girls, are most at risk during and after a crisis.

Please donate now to the Sudan: Children’s Emergency Appeal. Your donation can help us keep children safe and protected across the region.
